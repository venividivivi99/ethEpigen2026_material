-   Find and download from
    [ENCODE](https://www.encodeproject.org/search/?type=Experiment&control_type!=*&status=released)
    the peaks (i.e. bed-like format) for p300, H3K27ac and H3K27me3 in
    mouse embryonic stem cells (mESC) and in one differentiated tissue
    (heart or liver)
    -   when there are replicates, we recommend using the bed file
        denoted as “conservative IDR thresholded peaks”)
-   Compare the signals across the two tissues, answering the following
    questions:
    -   What is the proportion of TSS that overlap H3K27ac, H3K27me3, and the combination of both, in each tissue?
    -   What is the relationship between the 3 signals (p300, H3K27ac, and H3K27me3), and is it the same in both tissues?
    -   What is the relationship between regulatory elements active in mESC and in the differentiated tissue?
-   For each question, provide figures and/or numbers (e.g. proportions of
    overlap, etc.), and write a paragraph describing your observations and
    what you think they mean.
